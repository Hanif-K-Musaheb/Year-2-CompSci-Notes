**Oligopoly:** A market that is dominated by a small number of firms.

**Cartel:** A group of suppliers who try to act as if they were a monopoly.

Cartels tend to collapse and lose their power for three reasons:
 - Cheating by the cartel members
 - New entrants and demand response
 - Government prosecution and regulation

<img width="651" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/876f9056-5294-4a91-a01b-34a9d4ab4283" />

**Tacit collusion:** When firms limit competition with one another but do so without explicit agreement or communication

Important barriers to entry:
 - Control over a key resource or input
 - Economies of scale
 - Government barriers
